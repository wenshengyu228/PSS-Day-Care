Partnership Overview

Huada Transportation has been a trusted partner of Partners Senior Services since 2024, delivering safe, on-time transportation for senior members to our community center, medical appointments, and wellness activities throughout Philadelphia.

Community Mobility

Door-to-door pickup and drop-off for day-center participants within a 12-mile service radius.

Medical Appointments

Coordinated rides to routine health visits, specialist follow-ups, and pharmacy pickups.

Family Visits

Flexible shuttle options for family events, holidays, and community gatherings.

Senior-Focused Services

  • Accessible Vehicles: wheelchair lifts, low-floor access, and secure seating.
  • Trained Drivers: professional staff certified in senior transport and passenger assistance.
  • Real-Time Tracking: GPS dispatch and customer updates for safety and punctuality.
  • Flexible Scheduling: advance bookings, weekly routes, and same-day reservation windows.

Safety & Quality

Vehicle Maintenance – daily inspections and certified service checks.
Driver Screening – background checks, defensive driving, and sensitivity training.
Customer Care – dedicated coordinator for Partners Senior Services transportation requests.

Our Fleet

Modern, well-maintained buses designed to safely transport seniors with comfort and accessibility in mind.

Reliable vehicles equipped with wheelchair accessibility, air conditioning, and comfortable seating for all seniors.
