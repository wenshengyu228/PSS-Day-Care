About Our Program

Partners Senior Services has served Philadelphia seniors since 2010 with accredited care that balances independence, dignity, and community engagement. Our campus is licensed, insured, and staffed by experienced professionals.

Mission

To support seniors by providing reliable, compassionate day services that promote physical and emotional well-being.

Vision

A community where every older adult experiences connection, purpose, and access to essential health resources.

Values

Respect, integrity, safety, and continuous improvement in service delivery.

Our Core Services

Daily Meals

Chef-prepared breakfasts and lunches with options for therapeutic diets, diabetes, and heart health.

Health & Wellness

Nurse-led assessments, exercise sessions, health education, and medication review by licensed staff.

Transportation

Certified drivers provide safe pickup and drop-off within a 12-mile radius, included in membership.
